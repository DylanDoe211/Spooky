~New Stuff~

Spider Grotto Overhaul:
-Spider grotto biome generation has been improved, the caves are now way more open, structure generation is better, added some new lakes to the biome, and much more
-Added many new enemies in both prehardmode and hardmode in the spider grotto
-Added clusters of newly added mycelium grass, which give off faint white light and grow new red mushroom trees (Because spooky mod didnt have enough mushrooms already)
-Added many block resprites, new blocks, new ambient tiles/objects, and other stuff to enhance the spider grotto's aesthetic more
-Added new birch trees which drop ambient leaves that fall to the ground, and have their own new wood block and furniture set

Spider Grotto Spore Event:
-The sporestorm is a new natural weather event where fog and spores take over the grotto, which occurs randomly in hardmode
-During this event, a new set of mite enemies will begin spawning alongside the normal enemies
-The fog intensity increases over time, and when intense enough the spiders will stop spawning and only mites can be found
-Towards the end of the event, the fog intensity will decrease until the event actually ends
-Mite enemies drop a unique new bloom seed and a new material which can be used to craft lots of fun new items
-You can also find pieces of a new armor set growing on the ground while this event is happening, and a funny new pet

Spider War Event:
-New wave-based event in the spider grotto you can do after defeating golem
-There is a total of 5 unique minibosses in the event, each with various different attacks
-Has a total of 10 waves, and the final wave will consist of an onslaught of minibosses until you get 20 kills
-Each miniboss has a trophy and some unique weapon drops, with the drop rate being dependant on the wave (10% drop chance for items before the final wave, and during final wave you get guaranteed drops)
-The event will automatically count as complete just by making it to wave 10, so you do not have to continue past that unless you want to farm for all the loot

Old Hunter Overhaul:
-The old hunter was a lackluster town npc and has been changed to be more unique, as well as being moved to hardmode where the mod has less content
-Old hunter is no longer locked behind a scavenger hunt, and because of this the giant cobweb and all of the old hunter pieces have been removed from the mod
-Old hunter is now revived by finding his corpse in his house deep in the spider grotto, and bringing back his soul which has been locked away in the lower catacombs in a certain ghostly chest
-Old hunter no longer moves into houses like regular town npcs, you will now need to visit him in his ruined house at the bottom of the spider grotto after you have revived him
-In hardmode, you can find various rare powerful enemies that spawn in the different spooky mod biomes, which each drop a special item you can give to the old hunter for unique rewards
-These enemies do not work like little eye enemies as you do not need a spawn item, you can just find the enemies, kill them, and give the special items to the old hunter
-Old hunter will also give you hints on where you can find all of these enemies when you talk to him, if you have not completed that corresponding quest

Slingshots Overhaul:
-Slingshots are now a unique subclass of slow but high damage ranged weapons
-Slingshots no longer have a weird mechanic of being able to charge them up and let go earlier at the cost of less damage, they now just have set use speeds like other items but with a unique use animation
-There is now a variety of different slingshot ammos across the mod, with each one having unique properties/gimmicks that can be beneficial in certain situations
-Certain slingshots will also convert mossy pebbles (Which is the basic slingshot ammo) into their own unique projectile, similarly to how some vanilla ranged items convert their corresponding basic ammo into something new
-Slingshots also now have a cartoon hit effect when their projectiles hit an enemy, with a bigger special one for critical hits (This is just a visual effect, we thought it would be funny to add)

Dialogue Overhaul:
-The dialogue UI that was added and used for krampus in the last major update is now used across almost every friendly npc you can talk with in spooky mod
-Some of the dialogue in the mod has been re-written to be better and have interactions with your player responding to them just like with krampus
-As of now, little bone and the suspicious skeleton are the only ones left who dont use this system, as we have plans to rewrite their dialogue in the future when we have time

New Catacombs Improvements:
-The entire catacombs no longer consists of rooms that have the exact same layouts, the rooms themselves are still pre-built but the furniture, loot, and special rare rooms are all randomized now
-Even though the individual rooms are still pre-built, there is now more than double the number of room variants as there was before, so hopefully the catacombs feels a lot more natural with less repition of rooms
-The pathways throughout the catacombs have also been made to be more random, which makes the catacombs feel like a proper maze for players to explore instead of just being able to run across the whole thing horizontally
-Catacomb loot chests are also now scattered around the catacombs randomly instead of the catacombs only having 4 pre-made loot rooms in each layer
-All of the items that the old hunter used to sell are now catacomb loot chest items, some of which are in the upper catacombs and some in the lower catacombs
-Added some special old wood rooms to add extra variance in both layers, such as libraries, living quarters, and painting galleries can generate in place of regular rooms
-Added new boulder trap and venom dart trap rooms in the upper catacombs because its funny
-Catacomb loot chests are now locked and you need to get keys for them, which can be dropped by enemies, found in barrels, or dropped by breaking wooden caskets
-The wooden caskets you can find inside the catacombs now drop items similarly to pots, giving you some useful ammos, potions, and sometimes catacomb chest keys
-Added a bunch of cool and funny paintings you can find in both layers of the catacombs

Other New Stuff:
-Added a bunch of achievements, there are now achievements for things like defeating bosses, completing events, getting items, and discovering stuff in the world (More may be added in the future as well)
-Added new double-jump bottle and hermes boots alternatives to the underground spooky forest cabin chests to give you early game mobility (The hermes boots alternative can be used to make spectre boots too)
-Added new balloon and horseshoe balloon upgrades for the spooky forest double-jump bottle, and an upgrade for spooky forest hermes boots with krampus workshop moon shoes
-Added many new enemies to the spooky forest, including a bunch of new blood moon exclusive enemies
-Added 8 new sentient weapons
-Added eye block walls, eye bricks, eye brick walls, sclera blocks, and added a safe version of organic mush walls you can craft and build houses with
-Added new tar pits cactus block dropped by tar pit cactuses along with a new set of weapons and armor you can craft with them
-Added a raveyard monolith that allows you to activate the raveyard visual effects, and a spore monolith for the visual effects in the new spore event
-Added the old wood furniture tiles that were missing from the set which are the chandelier, candelabra, clock, dresser, lamp, lantern, and sink
-Added multiple new furniture sets which are the upper catacombs, lower catacombs, old birch wood, and tar pit cactus sets
-Added spider grotto fishing crates, and a hardmode version of the existing spooky forest crate
-Added new spider grotto angler quest fish
-Added tall kelp plants to the rotten depths
-Added glowing flower cactuses to the tar pits
-Added glowing radioactive bulbs to the fetid farms
-Added the sludge
